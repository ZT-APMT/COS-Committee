Education & Training

Empowering employees and their families through quality education and professional development opportunities

Objective To provide employees and their families with access to quality education and professional development opportunities through partnerships with language centers, schools, and training institutions, while offering financial support and resources that encourage continuous learning.

Program Description

This project aims to enhance learning opportunities for employees and their families by establishing partnerships with reputable institutions. It covers language learning through centers such as the American Language Center (English), Cervantes (Spanish), and Goethe (German). For employees, professional training will be offered in collaboration with local universities and business schools.
