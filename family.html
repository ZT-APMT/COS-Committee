Family & Social Benefits

Supporting work-life balance through comprehensive family-oriented programs

Objective To enhance employees' quality of life by providing family-oriented benefits such as housing and car purchase programs, seasonal allowances, and recreational opportunities, thereby strengthening work-life balance and employee satisfaction.

Program Description

The Family & Social Benefits project is designed to support employees' personal and family needs beyond the workplace. It includes programs for car and housing purchases, summer vacation packages (Estivage), and family operations.

üéÅ Seasonal Benefits Aid Al Adha allowance of +500 MAD and other seasonal benefits are provided to support families during important occasions.

Additionally, the committee is negotiating car purchase conventions with brands like Opel, KIA, Hyundai, and Renault to provide employees with better deals. This project reflects the company's commitment to improving the quality of life for employees and their families.
