How to Get Insurance Coverage

  1. Contact the COS Insurance team to learn about available coverage options
  2. For auto insurance: Reach out to Sanlam Agency with your COS membership details
  3. For life and travel insurance: Wait for provider confirmation announcements
  4. Complete required application forms and provide necessary documentation
  5. Review policy terms and premium rates before finalizing coverage
  6. Keep your policy documents safe and contact providers for claims assistance
