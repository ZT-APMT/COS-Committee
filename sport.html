Sports & Wellbeing

Promoting healthy lifestyles through fitness programs and wellness activities

Objective To promote a healthy and active lifestyle among employees by facilitating access to sports facilities, fitness programs, and wellness activities, fostering both physical health and social engagement within the company community.

Program Description

This project promotes a healthy lifestyle by encouraging physical activity and mental wellness. It includes partnerships with gyms, football centers, and recreational facilities across Tangier, T√©touan, and Fnideq.

Employees will have access to discounted memberships at popular gyms like Olympic, Energym, and FitOne, as well as opportunities to participate in football leagues, yoga sessions, biking events, and dance classes. The goal is to create a vibrant community where employees can stay active, reduce stress, and build social connections outside the workplace.
