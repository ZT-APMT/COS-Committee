Travel Programs

Cultural and leisure travel opportunities with preferential rates

Objective To organize cultural and leisure travel opportunities for employees, including Omra programs and local outings, while negotiating preferential rates with travel agencies, ferry companies, and hotels to make these experiences accessible and affordable.

Program Description

The Travel Project focuses on organizing cultural and adventure trips, as well as leisure activities, for employees. A key highlight is the Omra program, which will allow 10 employees to perform Omra during Ramadan, with a fair selection process and shift planning.

üïå Omra Program Highlights 10 employees will be selected to perform Omra during Ramadan (2 per shift + Admin). Fair selection process ensures equal opportunity for all eligible employees.

The project also includes partnerships with ferry companies like Balearia and AML for discounted travel, as well as agreements with hotels and guest houses for employee outings. This initiative is designed to strengthen team spirit and provide enriching experiences outside the workplace.
